Origin Story

We met in the ruins of a failed startup, three refugees from corporate creative departments seeking something harder to break.

Marcus brought the strategy. Sofia brought the vision. James brought the code. Together, we discovered a shared obsession with pressure—the belief that creative work only achieves its final form under specific, intense conditions.

Obsidian Prism Lab was born from this philosophy: that brands, like volcanic glass, must be forged in heat and cooled under pressure to achieve their sharpest, most durable form.

We don't have clients. We have collaborators willing to enter the furnace with us.

Our Covenant

Pressure Creates

We embrace constraint as fuel. Tight timelines, limited budgets, complex problems—these are the conditions where brilliance crystallizes.

Darkness First

We begin in the void. No preconceptions, no templates, no safe choices. Every project starts as a blank abyss we fill with intention.

Geometry Over Decoration

Form follows function follows feeling. Every element exists for a reason. Ornaments are stripped until only essential structure remains.

Transmutation Not Translation

We don't execute briefs. We transform them. The final work should be unrecognizable from the starting point—yet inevitable in retrospect.
